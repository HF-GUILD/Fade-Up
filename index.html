Welcome to our experiment

You will see 101 images arranged in a grid.

Primary Task

You will hear a short description of one image.
Scroll until you find the described image and then click on it once.
When you click on an image, it will be highlighted for just a moment.
Once you have clicked on an image, you will hear the next description.
Please scroll, find, and click on that image.
The process will repeat 19 times.
Please be as accurate as possible.
Please go as quickly as you can without making mistakes.
